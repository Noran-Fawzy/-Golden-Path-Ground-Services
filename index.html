Company Overview

Golden Path Ground Services is a premier provider of comprehensive airport ground handling operations, dedicated to supporting airline partners with exceptional service quality and operational excellence. Our organization operates under strict adherence to International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) standards and implements a robust Safety Management System (SMS) that ensures the highest levels of safety, efficiency, and reliability.
With a team of highly trained professionals and state-of-the-art equipment, we deliver a full spectrum of ground support services including aircraft handling, passenger services, baggage handling, cargo operations, and ramp services. Our commitment to safety is embedded in every aspect of our operations, creating a culture where safety excellence drives continuous improvement and operational success.
